# Prompt Templates: Meeting Preparation

Ready-to-use prompts for preparing for meetings and standups.

---

## Daily Standup Prep

> Prepare my daily standup update:
> 1. What was completed yesterday? (issues closed, PRs merged)
> 2. What's in progress today? (open issues assigned to team members)
> 3. Any blockers? (failing CI, stale PRs, unresolved issues)

## Sprint Review Prep

> Prepare materials for sprint review:
> - List all issues completed this sprint with brief descriptions
> - Show demo-ready features (merged PRs with "enhancement" label)
> - Summarize velocity and burndown metrics
> - List items that spilled over to the next sprint

## Sprint Planning Prep

> Help me prepare for sprint planning:
> - Show the current backlog prioritized by importance
> - Calculate our average velocity from the last 3 sprints
> - Suggest a realistic number of issues for the next sprint
> - Flag any dependencies between issues

## 1:1 Meeting Prep

> Prepare notes for my 1:1 with [TEAM_MEMBER]:
> - What have they been working on? (their recent issues and PRs)
> - What's their workload like? (open issues assigned to them)
> - Any blockers they're facing?
> - Recognition: what did they ship recently?

## Stakeholder Meeting Prep

> Prepare talking points for my stakeholder meeting:
> - Overall project health (on track, at risk, behind)
> - Key accomplishments this period
> - Upcoming milestones and dates
> - Decisions or approvals needed
> - Risks and mitigation plans

## Retrospective Facilitation

> Help me facilitate our sprint retrospective:
> - Pull objective data: velocity, cycle time, PR review time, CI pass rate
> - Compare with previous sprint
> - Identify patterns: what's improving? what's getting worse?
> - Suggest 3 discussion topics based on the data

## Cross-Team Sync Prep

> Prepare for our cross-team sync meeting:
> - What has our team shipped since the last sync?
> - What are we working on next?
> - Any cross-team dependencies or blockers?
> - Questions for other teams

## WorkIQ Meeting Context

> Using WorkIQ, help me prepare for my upcoming meeting:
> - What emails have I received about [TOPIC] this week?
> - What was discussed in the last meeting about [TOPIC]?
> - Any relevant documents shared on Teams or SharePoint?
> - Who are the key people involved?
